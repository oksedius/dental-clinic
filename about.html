About our clinic

Welcome to the dental clinic!

Whether you are interested in a specific dental treatment or need a general examination and consultation, our team is ready to provide you with quality medical care in a modern dental center.

Our dental clinic has a wide range of high-quality dental services (diagnosis and therapy, pediatric dentistry and surgery, prosthetics and implantation, orthodontics, vector therapy, aesthetic dentistry, and others) that will help you control your oral health.

Our experience and equipment

Our dental clinic offers fully functional offices on the ground floor and wheelchair access to ensure your maximum comfort during your dental visit. We use the many years of experience of our team and highly qualified dentists of various specialties.

We also use state-of-the-art technology such as computed tomography and intraoral scanning. With the help of CT, we conduct diagnostics, discuss all problematic issues related to the oral cavity. Using a scanner, we can digitally make a detailed 3D model of the jaw for a quick and accurate impression. It is ideal for crowns, bridges, implants and orthodontic caps. You can count on urgent dental care - because the appointment is also open in the evening hours and on weekends.

Algorithm for drawing up a dental treatment plan:

1. After the consultation and diagnostic CT scan, the doctor will prepare several treatment options for you - a treatment plan.

2. Next, you can choose the plan that suits you best, discuss the relevant details and timing of treatment.

3. Once you and our dentist have determined which plan is best for maintaining your oral health, our treatment coordinator will help you make appointments.

Please call the dental clinic at (067) 572 84 73 to make an appointment or consultation. Our friendly receptionist will be happy to answer any questions you may have about our services.
